Tips to Protect yourself!

WEAR MASK

Wearing mask is very essential for you as well as the people near you. It filters the coronavirus. But it is necessary to wear a mask advised by a doctor else it will be of no use against coronavirus

WASH YOUR HANDS

It is also advised that one should wash his hands in a particular interval of time. Always try not to touch your face as there is high chance of spreading coronavirus to your body.

SANITIZE YOUR HANDS

Sanitizing hands is a good practise. It will kill coronavirus as well as other germs which can harm your immunity. It is also advised that with hands one should also sanitize the things which are frequently in use.

Most common symptoms

  • Fever
  • Dry cough
  • Tiredness

Less common symptoms

  • Aches and pains
  • Sore throat
  • Diarrhoea
  • Conjunctivitis
  • Headache
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • A rash on skin, or discolouration of fingers or toes

Serious symptoms

  • Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • Chest pain or preasure
  • Loss of speech or movement

Do's

  • Seek immediate medical attention if you have serious symptoms. Always call before visiting your doctor or health facility.
  • People with mild symptoms who are otherwise healthy should manage their symptoms at home.

Dont's

  • Do not remove your mask in public places to click photographs.
  • Do not go to office if you are experiencing symptoms of COVID-19. Also, inform your manager.
  • Do not share personal belongings like phone chargers, stationery, make-up accessories, etc. In case you really have to share, ensure to sanitise the items before and after use.
